  - **Order & Shipment Tracking**
    - From inquiry → quotation → order → shipment → delivery
    - Real-time status updates
    - Document management (invoices, certificates, bills of lading)
  
  - **Lead Management**
    - Capture leads from website, ads, trade shows
    - Automatic assignment to sales team
    - Follow-up reminders
    - Conversion tracking
  
  - **Multi-Currency & Multi-Country Support**
    - Handle USD, EUR, AED, CNY, etc.
    - Country-specific regulations
    - Time zone management
  
  - **Analytics Dashboard**
    - Sales by country
    - Top products export data
    - Revenue trends
    - Buyer behavior insights

**Technology:** FastAPI (Python) + PostgreSQL + React
**Timeline:** 8-10 weeks
