India's Premier Tuna Supplier

We are India's leading supplier of premium Yellowfin tuna, serving restaurants, hotels, and seafood distributors across the country. Our commitment to quality and sustainability sets us apart in the industry.

With over a decade of experience in the seafood industry, we've built a reputation for delivering the freshest, highest-quality tuna year-round. Our advanced cold chain management ensures that every piece of fish reaches you in perfect condition, maintaining the premium quality from ocean to table.

What makes us special: We're the only supplier in India offering Yellowfin tuna 365 days a year, even during off-season. Our sustainable sourcing practices and direct relationships with fisheries guarantee premium quality at competitive prices. We work with certified sustainable fisheries and maintain strict quality control at every step.

Our Promise: Every order is handled with care, flash-frozen at peak freshness, and delivered with temperature-controlled logistics to ensure you receive restaurant-grade tuna every single time.
