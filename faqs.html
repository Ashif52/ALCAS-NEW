General Inquiries
What services does ALCAS Digital provide?
We provide a comprehensive suite of digital solutions including custom website development, CRM implementation, digital marketing (SEO, PPC, Social Media), branding, and business automation strategies.
How long does a typical website project take?
The timeline varies based on complexity. A standard business website usually takes 4-6 weeks, while more complex e-commerce or custom web applications can take 8-12 weeks from strategy to launch.
Do you provide customized pricing for startups?
Yes, we understand that every business has different needs. We offer flexible packages and can create custom proposals tailored to the specific budget and goals of startups and small businesses.
Will my website be mobile-friendly?
Absolutely. Every website we build is "mobile-first" and fully responsive, ensuring it looks and performs perfectly on smartphones, tablets, and desktops.
Do you offer post-launch support and maintenance?
Yes, we provide ongoing maintenance plans that include security updates, performance optimization, content updates, and technical support to ensure your digital assets continue to perform at their best.
How do you measure the success of marketing campaigns?
We use data-driven metrics including conversion rates, ROI, traffic growth, and engagement levels. We provide monthly transparent reports so you can see exactly how your investment is performing.
Can you help with rebranding an existing business?
Yes, rebranding is one of our core specialties. We help businesses modernize their visual identity, refine their messaging, and relaunch with a professional, cohesive brand strategy.
What makes ALCAS different from other agencies?
We don't just offer services; we build systems. Our focus is on digital transformation—integrating marketing, sales, and automation into a unified ecosystem that actually scales your business.
Do you work with clients outside of Chennai?
Yes, we work with businesses globally. While our headquarters is in Chennai, our digital-first workflow allows us to collaborate effectively with clients across India and internationally.
How do I get started with a project?
It's simple! You can click the "Get Started" button on our website or visit our Contact Page to book a free discovery call where we'll discuss your goals and how we can help.
